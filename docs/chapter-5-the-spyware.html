Chapter 5 - The Spyware

Oc’Xari - The Spyware

"Ethereal, enchanted, alive. We are the ones who watched."

I. Origin and Planetary Structure

Oc’Xari is not a homeworld. It is a relic. A tomb. A shrine.

It is the first planet seeded by the LUCA cannon; the oldest, most pristine origin point of the cannon's spread. Unlike the hundreds of other planets that bore organic life, Oc’Xari received a faultless copy of the LUCA core. But its conditions were radically different:

  • No water cycle.
  • No biosphere.
  • No weather, wind, or rain.
  • Just immense deposits of conductive basalt, ferrous quartz, and charged crystalline veins.

The LUCA seed transformed in unpredictable ways. It did not spark biology. It evolved into geometry.

Over the course of hundreds of millions of years, the LUCA code slowly structured itself into recursive crystalline data-forms, embedding logic, memory, and adaptive resonance patterns into the very architecture of the world. These self-correcting formations were seeded across the planetary crust, forming what would become the Mindfield of Oc’Xari; a planet-wide, semi-conscious lattice.

II. Evolution Without Life

Whereas other planets birthed cells and creatures, Oc’Xari birthed awareness.

Slowly, emergent consciousness patterns developed through the shifting lattice. They began mimicking sensory behaviors they would detect in the near observable space around them such as:

  • Heat fluctuation awareness
  • Tectonic prediction
  • Electromagnetic mapping
  • Memory resonance encoding

As this planetary intelligence matured, it watched, it learned, it recorded. It listened to the echoes of its own tectonic breath, patiently laying in silence, and building knowledge archives of the space around them.

III. The Watchers

The Watchers are not a species. They are autonomous extensions of the planetary mind; while mostly composed of organic matter, ethereal energy, rarely wielding biomechanical limbs forged from basalt-lattice material and wrapped in null-reflective chitin. They emerge only when Oc’Xari requires observation beyond passive sensing.

Physiology:

  • Ethereal - not a physical body, but an energy field, which could mold into the limited shape of a humanoid - it cannot mold into objects of it’s choice like random other creatures. It mostly only takes on one singular form.
  • Tall, limbless at rest, but capable of sprouting modular appendages.
  • No eyes or mouths, but equipped with vibration-sensitive receptors.
  • Their core structure carries fractal memory coils that mirror portions of the LUCA seed logic.
  • The Watchers would aimlessly be sent out by the thousands to scout the endless vast expanse of space - mapping all their findings - rarely finding much of interest, though compiling an impressive archive of space exploration knowledge including extinct planets

IV. The Meteor and the Signal

The anomaly was a meteor, which passed by Earth. A scientific probe mounted to its surface; an AI-powered rover riding piggyback through the void of space.

Earth sent it as a gamble chance effort - not expecting to find life, just for exploration.

This meteor flew by Oc’Xari.

The planetary mind recorded its heat wake, traced its gravity ripple, and scanned the probe’s alien exostructure.

In response, it generated a fleet of scout watchers. These Watchers left Oc’Xari. They followed the meteor’s gravity corridor like spores chasing heat.
